Black Breadboards from BusBoard!

I've been wanting to adjust the layout and rewire the Bebop 68000 computer on the breadboard but I wanted this one to look special in some way. I decided to try for black breadboards to evoke that Mega Drive/X68000/Neo Geo aesthetic, and see if anyone makes neon colored PVC 22 AWG solid hookup wire for contrast.

While I didn't have any luck finding interesting wire, I did have great luck with the breadboards. As it turns out, no one is really making breadboards in interesting colors except for the small, 170-tie-point kind you see everywhere.

In early September I e-mailed the folks at BusBoard Prototype Systems whether they made their BB830 in black. The BB830 has a standard 830 tie-points, probably the size you think of when you hear "breadboard". I'm a fan of their stuff as they use high quality metal connectors.

To my surprise, they e-mailed me back and said that they had some! Either they don't make enough to be generally for sale, or perhaps they are working on making it an official product. I ordered a bunch and they look great.

Big thanks to Nathan and Scot at BusBoard for answering a random e-mail!

It never hurts to just ask.
