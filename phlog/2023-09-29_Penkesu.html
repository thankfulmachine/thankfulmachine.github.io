Penkesu Mini PC

I've been looking for a really small cyberdeck-like computer to keep in my bag and I found something interesting. It could be a good starting point for some modifications.

It's called the Penkesu from one Penk Chen on GitHub. It's simple and made of contempory and readily available components. I especially like the clever use of Gameboy Advance SP replacement hinges. 606090 lithium ion battery, Adafruit PowerBoost, mechanical keys, QMK-based keyboard firmware, and lots of other well curated components. There's a lot to like about it.

The one thing I really like about the cyberdeck and DIY keyboard communities is the utter fearlessness in throwing things together. That's how I'm trying to be.
