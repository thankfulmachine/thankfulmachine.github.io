Rewiring the Bebop 68000

I'm creating a "revision 2" of the Bebop 68000 with two main changes:

  • Removing the address and data bus transceivers as everything is CMOS with very high input impedance. I didn't notice any drops when removing them in the revision 1 board.
  • Adding a Dallas DS1510 real-time clock for fun

Hopefully, with a slightly more compact layout, I can get the wires even neater than before. With the smart black breadboards I got from BusBoard, I'm looking forward to making the best handmade revision of this computer yet.
