Deciding on Specs

Right, so I decided on this basic starting architecture for my machine:

  • 68000 at 8-12MHz
  • 1MB RAM
  • 256K ROM
  • Two 68681 DUARTS, providing 4 serial ports and plenty of parallel GPIO
  • Perhaps other peripherals like a real-time clock and FPGA A/V (for the future)

I wanted to make all of this by hand on a breadboard, so the memory choices are partly to do with the availability of through-hole parts and a light adherence to the specs at the time. Here are some approximate specs that I collected from Wikipedia on some memorable machines to make my decision.

These numbers are approximate! No need to panic.
